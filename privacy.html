Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

PuzzleVerse is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you visit PuzzleVerse, we may automatically collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website (how you found us)
  • General geographic location (country/city level only)

We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifying information unless you voluntarily provide it via our contact form.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Understand which games are most popular
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Display relevant advertisements (via Google AdSense)

3. Google AdSense & Cookies

PuzzleVerse uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information on how Google uses data, visit: Google Privacy Policy.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects anonymous usage data. No personally identifiable information is collected. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. PuzzleVerse uses cookies to:

  • Save your game scores and preferences
  • Enable advertising features via Google AdSense
  • Analyze website traffic via Google Analytics

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of some games.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

PuzzleVerse is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected on our site. Our website uses HTTPS encryption to secure all data transmission between your browser and our servers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Website: puzzleverse-byme.netlify.app/contact.html
  • Via our Contact Page
